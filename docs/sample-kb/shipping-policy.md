# Shipping Policy

## Delivery times
Standard shipping within India takes 3-5 business days. Express shipping takes 1-2 business days and is available for an additional fee of ₹150.

International orders ship to the US, UK, Canada, and Australia, and typically take 7-12 business days depending on customs processing.

## Order tracking
Every order receives a tracking link by email within 24 hours of dispatch. You can also track your order from the "My Orders" section of your account by entering your order ID.

## Shipping costs
Orders above ₹999 within India ship free. Orders below this threshold have a flat shipping fee of ₹79. International shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on destination and package weight.

## Delayed or lost packages
If a package hasn't moved for more than 5 business days, contact support with your order ID and we'll open an investigation with the courier. If a package is confirmed lost, we issue a full refund or a free reshipment, whichever the customer prefers.

## Address changes
Address changes are only possible before an order has shipped. Once a tracking number has been generated, we're unable to redirect the package, and the customer will need to coordinate directly with the courier or wait for the package to be returned to us.
