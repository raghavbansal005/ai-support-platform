# Pricing & Plans FAQ

## Available plans
- **Starter** - ₹0/month. Good for trying the product. Limited to 50 orders/month and email support only.
- **Growth** - ₹2,499/month. Up to 2,000 orders/month, priority email support, and access to the loyalty points program.
- **Scale** - ₹7,999/month. Unlimited orders, dedicated account manager, live chat + phone support, and API access.

All plans are billed monthly and can be upgraded or downgraded at any time from Account Settings > Billing. Downgrades take effect at the start of the next billing cycle; upgrades take effect immediately with a prorated charge.

## Payment methods
We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI, and net banking for Indian customers, and credit cards or PayPal for international customers.

## Annual billing discount
Paying annually instead of monthly gives a 15% discount on the Growth and Scale plans. This can be selected during checkout or by contacting billing support to switch an existing subscription.

## Cancellations
Plans can be cancelled anytime from Account Settings > Billing > Cancel Plan. Access continues until the end of the current billing period; there are no cancellation fees and no partial refunds for the current cycle.

## Failed payments
If a monthly payment fails, we retry automatically 3 times over 5 days. If all retries fail, the account is downgraded to the Starter plan and the customer is notified by email. Repeated payment failures on the same account should be flagged for a human to review, since it may indicate a card issue the customer isn't aware of.
