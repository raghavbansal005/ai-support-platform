# Account & General FAQ

## Creating an account
An account is created automatically at first checkout using the email provided. A password can be set afterward from the confirmation email link, or a customer can continue checking out as a guest on future orders.

## Resetting a password
Use the "Forgot password" link on the login page. A reset link is emailed and expires after 30 minutes for security. If the email doesn't arrive within a few minutes, check spam, then contact support to verify the account email on file.

## Updating account details
Name, email, phone number, and saved addresses can all be updated from Account Settings > Profile. Email changes require confirming the new address via a verification link before it takes effect.

## Order history
All past orders are visible under "My Orders," including status, tracking, and invoices. Invoices can be downloaded as a PDF directly from the order detail page.

## Data & privacy requests
Customers can request a copy of their data or full account deletion by emailing privacy@acmecorp.example. These requests are processed within 30 days as required by applicable data protection law. Account deletion is irreversible and should always be confirmed explicitly with the customer before proceeding, and any request that sounds like it involves a legal or regulatory complaint should be escalated to a human immediately.

## Contacting support
Live chat (this assistant) is available 24/7 for general questions. For anything the assistant can't resolve, a ticket is created automatically and a human follows up by email, usually within one business day.
