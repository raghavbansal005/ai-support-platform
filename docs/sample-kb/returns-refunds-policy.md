# Returns & Refunds Policy

## Return window
Items can be returned within 15 days of delivery, provided they're unused, in original packaging, and have all tags attached. Sale items and personalized/custom products are final sale and cannot be returned.

## How to start a return
Go to "My Orders," select the item, and choose "Request Return." A prepaid return label is emailed within a few hours. Drop the package at any courier partner location within 7 days of requesting the return.

## Refund timing
Once the returned item passes inspection at our warehouse (usually 2-3 business days after we receive it), the refund is issued to the original payment method. Refunds typically appear within 5-7 business days depending on the bank.

## Exchanges
We currently support exchanges only for size or color, and only for the same product. Request an exchange the same way as a return, then choose "Exchange" instead of "Refund" at checkout.

## Damaged or incorrect items
If an item arrives damaged or the wrong item was sent, contact support with a photo within 48 hours of delivery. These cases skip the standard return window and are eligible for a full refund or free replacement, shipped at our cost.

## Refund escalations
If a refund hasn't posted after 10 business days from warehouse confirmation, this should be escalated to a human agent, since it usually indicates a payment gateway issue that needs manual reconciliation.
